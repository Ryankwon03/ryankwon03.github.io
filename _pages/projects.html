Reordered Noise Processing in Super Resolution

(EECS 442) This project proposes a two‑stage restoration pipeline—applying diffusion‑based 4× super‑resolution before denoising—that markedly enhances the fidelity of images corrupted by multiple noise and downsampling operations compared with denoising alone.
